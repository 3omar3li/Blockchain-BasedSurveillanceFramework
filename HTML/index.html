Blockchain-Based Surveillance Framework


Our project to achieve these objectives:


Creating a permissioned Blockchain for surveillance stream data to be stored securely
Authorized users can access their surveillance streams and associated metadata
A system that has the ability to record and livestream ongoing events
Scalable system that can be produced broadly on a smart city
Full Recorded footage accessibility for different areas will be on local storages
